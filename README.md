# YNJ Vend

## Overview

YNJ Vend is a cloud-based inventory management and customer relationship management (CRM) application built for beverage distributors. The platform helps distributors track inventory levels, manage convenience store customers, and automate common business processes using AWS serverless services.

---

## Business Problem

YNJ Vending distributors rely on spreadsheets, handwritten inventory counts, and manual customer tracking. This can lead to:

* Inventory shortages
* Missed sales opportunities
* Inaccurate inventory counts
* Poor customer follow-up
* Time-consuming administrative work

YNJ Vend APP modernizes these processes with a cloud-native solution.

---

## Current Features

### Inventory Management

* Inventory database using Amazon DynamoDB
* Product quantity tracking
* Low inventory threshold support
* Scalable serverless backend

### Customer CRM

* Store information management
* Contact information tracking
* Customer status tracking
* Customer notes
* Last order tracking
